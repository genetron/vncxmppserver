TightVNC Donations
 

If you use and like TightVNC, please consider making a donation. There are several possible methods to do it:

Credit card payment via the Kagi Internet store (minimal amount is $10). Click the logo image at the left to go to the payment page at Kagi (opens a new window).
Payment via PayPal, a popular online payment system. Click the logo image at the left to go to the payment page at PayPal, or just direct your payment to the address donate at tightvnc.com.
Also, you can send a check with the text "TightVNC" in the MEMO field, to the following address:
Aleksandr Tarabykin
P.O. Box 2555
Westwood MA 02090
USA
Finally, there is an option to donate a book via the Amazon wish list page.

If you have made a donation recently, your e-mail messages will be read and answered before processing the rest of e-mail. Even if we are overloaded with mail, we can see that there are unread messages from donors, whose e-mail addresses correspond to those specified in the payment details.

Thank you in advance!
